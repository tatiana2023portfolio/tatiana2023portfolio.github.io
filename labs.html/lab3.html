Recipe card: Chocolate chip cookies

How to make cookies Ingredients you need *Eggs *Flour *Milk *Chocolate chips *Butter Steps to make them; Step 1: Add 2 cups of flour, then crack 2 eggs Step 2: Put 1 chunk of butter, then put 1 cup of milk Step 3: Mix. Whlie mixing add chocolate chips and continue mixing until dough formed. Step 4: Knead to a shape of a cookie. Step 5: Put the cookies in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes, and take them of if you think they are done. Now you're ready to make cookies and impress your friends and family.
